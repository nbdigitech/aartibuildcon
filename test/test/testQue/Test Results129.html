    2.   L={ai bj ck |i=j or j=k}
    3.   L={ai bj ck |i≠j and j≠k}
      Explanation:
      The given grammar G is
      S →AX | YC
      A→aA | ϵ
      B→bB | ϵ
      C→cC | ϵ
      X→bXc | bB |cC
      Y→aYb | aA |bB

      The production X→bXc | bB |cC counts number of b’s and c’s and makes sure that either there is at least one more b than the number of c’s or at least one more c than the number of b’s. See that no b’s appear after c’s. X is in turn derived from the production S →AX.
      Similarly, the productions Y→aYb | aA |bB counts number of a’s and b’s and makes sure that either there is at least one more a than the number of b’s or at least one more b than the number of a’s. See that no a’s appear after b’s. X is in turn derived from the production S →YC.
      The symbols A,B and C generate arbitrary number of a’s, b’s and c’s, respectively.
      So, the language is L={ai bj ck |i≠j and j≠k}
