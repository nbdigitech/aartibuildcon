      Given x/y=2/1⇒x=2k,y=k
      2k-k=4
      k=4
      Then, x=8,y=4
      And Number =10×8+4=84

  21. A ball is dropped from a height of 108 m. The ball rebounds each time to a height equal to 1/3 rd of the height of previous bounce. The approximate distance travelled by the ball till it stops bouncing is( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   162 m
    2.   180 m
    3.   216 m
      Explanation:

      Initially,the ball is dropped from 108 m.
      After hitting the floor,it rebounds to a height of 1/3×108=36 m
      Distance covered in 1st bounce=36+36=72 m
      (Ball goes up 36m and comes down 36m for the second bounce)
      Distance covered in 2nd bounce=2×1/3×1/3×108
      (∵Both up & down journey)
      Distance covered in 3rd bounce=2×1/3×1/3×1/3×108
      ⇒Distance covered in nth bounce=2×1/3n ×108
      ∴Total distance travelled by the ball
      =108+2×108(1/3+1/32 +1/33 + ……)
      =108+2×108((1/3)/(1-1/3))
      =108+2×108×1/2
      ≅216m
    4.   240 m
