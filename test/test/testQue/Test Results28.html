  8. In the group G={2,4,6,8} under multiplication modulo 10, the inverse of element 2 is __________( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   8
      Explanation:
      Let ‘a’ be any element belongs to the group G.
      If a*e=a, then ‘e’ is an identity element.
      The given group G={2,4,6,8} and the binary operation * is multiplication modulo 10.
      ⇒2⊗10 6=2
      4⊗10 6=2
      6⊗10 6=6
      8⊗10 6=8
      ∴ 6 is an identity element.
      Let a, b are the elements belong to group G.
      If a*b=e then ‘b’ is the inverse of ‘a’.
      ⇒2⊗10 b=6
      ∴b=8
      So, inverse of element 2 is 8.
    2.   6
    3.   4
    4.   2
