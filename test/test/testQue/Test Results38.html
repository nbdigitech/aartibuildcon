  11. Two numbers are such that their difference, their sum and their product are to one another as 1:5:12. The product of the two numbers is( Marks: 0.00 )

      2x=6a
      x=3a

      ∴ Product of the two numbers =xy=12a=24

  22. P and Q working alone can complete a piece of work in 10 and 15 days respectively. They start working together, on 4th day Q is replaced by R. If the work is finished in next 4 days, then in how many days can R alone finish the work?( Marks: 0.00 )

      ∴ x = 3
      R’s 1 day work = 3 units/ day
      For 120 units, he takes 120/3=40 days

  28. A bag contains 5 white and 3 black balls; another bag contains 4 white and 5 black balls. From any one of these bags a single draw of two balls is made. Find the probability that one of them would be white and other black ball.( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   275/504
      Explanation:
      The probability of choosing either bag is1/2.
      From first bag,
      Number of ways of drawing two balls =8C2 =28
      Number of ways of drawing 1 white & 1 black ball = 5C1X3C1 = 15
      Hence, probability that of the two balls drawn, 1 is white and 1 is black is 15/28.
      Second bag,
      Number of ways of drawing two balls = 9C2 = 36
      Number of ways of drawing 1 white & 1 black ball = 4C1X5C1 = 20
      Hence, probability that of the two balls drawn, 1 is white and 1 is black is 20/36.
      ∴Required probability=1/2×15/28+1/2×20/36
      =275/504

    3.   5/9

  29. Three persons A, B and C are given a work. B can do the work in 32 days. If A is 22% more efficient than B and C is 25% more efficient than A, then A and C together can finish the work in approximately.( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   12 days
      Explanation:
      Let Work to be done =32x units
      B can do the work in 32 days
      ⇒∴B^' s efficiency=x units/ day
      ⇒A^' s efficiency=x(1+22/100) units/day
      =122x/100 units/day
      =1.22x units/day
      ⇒C^' s efficiency=1.22x(1+25/100) units/day
      =1.22x(125/100) units/day
      =1.525x units/day
      Total work done by A and C in 1 day=1.22x+1.525x
      =2.745x
      ⇒A and C can finish the work in 32x/2.745x days
      ≅12 days (approximately)
