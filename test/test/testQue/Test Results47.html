  10. There are 3 temples in a village and a well in front of each temple. A pilgrim came to the village with certain number of flowers. He washed all the flowers he had with the water of well in front of first temple. To his surprise flowers doubled. He devoted few flowers to the god in the first temple and moved to the second temple. He again washed the remaining flowers and again became doubled. He devoted few flowers to the god in the second temple and moved to the third temple.Here also his flowers doubled after washing them with water. He devoted few flowers to the god in third temple.
    There were no flowers left when pilgrim came out of the third temple and he offered same number of flowers to the god in all three temples. What is the minimum number of flowers the pilgrim had initially?( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   8
    2.   16
    3.   14
    4.   7
      Explanation:
      Explanation:
      Let 'x' be the number of flowers the pilgrim had initially
      Let 'y' be the number of flowers devoted at each temple

      At temple 1:
      Let 'p' be the number of flowers left
      Thus
      2x - y= p-----------1
      At temple 2:
      Number of flowers the pilgrim has after devoting at temple 1 =p
      Let 'q' be the number of flowers left
      Thus
      2p-y=q ------------------2

      At temple 3:
      Number of flower the pilgrim has after devoting at temple 2 =q

      After devoting at temple3 he doesn't have any flower.
      2q-y=0 -------------3
      => q=y/2
      Substitute the value of 'q' in ---2
      => 2p-y =q
      => 2p-y =y/2
      =>2p=3y/2 => p=3y/4
      Substitute the value of 'p' in -----1
      2x-y=3y/4
      =>2x=7y/4
      => x= 7y/8
      => 8x=7y, and
      The least possible solution for this is x=7 & y=8
      So initially pilgrim has 7 flowers and devoted 8 flowers at each temple.

  13. Based on given statements, select the most appropriate option to solve the given question. The towns A, B, C are in a straight line. Town C is between A and B. The distance from A to B is 100Km. How far is A from C?
    Statements:
    I) The distance from A to B is 25% more than the distance from C to B.
    II) The distance from A to C is 1/4 of the distance from C to B.( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   Statement I alone is sufficient, but statement II alone is not sufficient.
    2.   Statement II alone is sufficient, but statement I alone is not sufficient
    3.   Either statement I or statement II alone is sufficient.
      Explanation:

      Let AC = xKM
      CB =(100-x) KM

      Statement I:
      The distance of AB =125% of distance CB
      => 100= 125/100 X (100-x)
      => 100-x = (100x-100)/125
      => 100-x =80
      => x= 20KM
      The distance from A to C = x = 20KM
      Thus,
      Statement I alone is sufficient to answer the question.

      Statement II:
      The distance from A to C =1/4 of the distance from C to B
      AC =1/4 CB
      x= 1/4(100-x)
      5x=100
      x=20KM
      ∴The distance from A to C = x=20KM
      Thus,
      Statement II alone is sufficient to answer the question.
      ∴So, either statement I or statement II alone is sufficient.
    4.   Both statements together are sufficient, but neither statement alone is sufficient.


  14. At a certain party, first the ratio of boys to girls was 9:7. If after 20 boys left, the ratio becomes 1:1, how many people were originally at the party?( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   80
    2.   112
    3.   144
    4.   160
      Explanation:
      Explanation:
      The ratio of boys to girls is 9:7
      That means,
      Number of boys= 9x
      Number of girls =7x
      Total number of people = 9x+7x
      =16x
      If 20 boys left, then the ratio becomes 1:1
      i.e. (9x-20)/7x =1/1
      9x-20=7x
      9x-7x=20
      2x=20
      x=10
      ∴Total number of people who were originally at the party = 16x
      =16 x 10 =160
