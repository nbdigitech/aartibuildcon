  28. Floating point numbers are represented in the way given below.

    with explicit Normalization.
    What is the maximum error in the range of representable numbers?( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   231
    2.   230
    3.   2(-9)
    4.   221
      Explanation:

      Max error = (I max – II max)/ 2
      Note: This is in range of representable numbers.
      Ist max=(-1)0 (0.111111111)2×2(63-32)
      =(0.111111111)2×231
      IInd max=(0.111111110)2×2(63-32)
      =(0.111111110)2×231
      Error=[(0.111111111)2 231-(0.111111110)2 231] /2
      =(0.111111111-0.111111110)×230
      =(0.000000001)2×230
      =2(-9)×230=221
