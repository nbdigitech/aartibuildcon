  8. If A2x2 is a matrix with | A | = 9 and tr (A) = 10 then what are the Eigen values of matrix A?

    (Note: tr(A) = Sum of all the diagonal elements of A)( Marks: 2.00 )
    1.   6,4
    2.   5,5
    3.   9,1
      Explanation:
      Given A2x2 is a matrix.

      Let λ1, λ2 are Eigen values of matrix A of order 2, then

      i) Sum of all Eigenvalues = tr(A) = 10
      ∴λ1 + λ2 = 10 ------ eq1

      ii) Product of all the Eigenvalues = | A | = 9
      ∴λ1 . λ2 = 9 -------- eq2

      (λ1 - λ2 )2 = (λ1 + λ2 )2 - 4 λ1 λ2
      = (10)2 – 4 (9)
      = 100 - 36
      = 64

  11. The Eigenvectors of a real symmetric matrix corresponding to different Eigen values are( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   singular
    2.   orthogonal
      Explanation:
      Let ‘ A ‘ be a real symmetric matrix
      AT=A
      Let λ1 and λ2 be different eigen values of the matrix A.
      Let X1 and X2 be the corresponding eigen vectors for λ1 and λ2, then
      A X1= λ1 X1--------------------1
      And
      A X2= λ2 X2--------------------2
      By taking transpose of the second equation
      (A X2)T= (λ2 X2)T
      X2TAT= λ2X2T
      X2TA= λ2X2T (since AT=A)
      Post multiply by X1,we get
      X2TAX1= λ2X2TX1
      X2T λ1X1= λ2X2TX1 (from equation 1)
      λ1 X2T X1= λ2X2TX1
      (λ1- λ2) X2T X1=0
      (λ1- λ2)≠0 because the Eigen values are different
      So, X2T X1=0
      i.e, The Eigen vectors X2 and X1 are orthogonal
    3.   non-singular
    4.   none of these
