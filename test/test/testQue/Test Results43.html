  12. An integer is chosen at random from the numbers 1,2,3,….25. The probability that the chosen number is divisible by 3 or 4, is( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   11/25
    2.   12/25
      Explanation:
      Explanation:
      Numbers divisible by 3 are 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, 21, 24.
      Total 8 numbers are divisible by 3.
      Numbers divisible by 4 are 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24
      Total 6 numbers are divisible by 4.
      Numbers divisible by 3 and 4 are 12, 24.
      Total 2 numbers are divisible by 3 and 4.
      Therefore, probability that the chosen number is divisible by 3 or 4 is
      P(3 or 4) = P(3) + P(4) – P(3 and 4)
    3.   13/25
    4.   14/25


  13. Mr. Ravi has 25 horses, and he wants to pick the fastest 3 horses out of those 25. He has only 5 tracks that means only 5 horses can run at a time and he doesn’t have a stop watch. What is the minimum number of races required to find the 3 fastest horses?( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   6
    2.   7
      Explanation:
      Let the horses be numbered as H1, H2, H3, …., H25. Now first arrange 5 races among horses as:
      Race 1: H1, H2, H3, H4, H5 ( Let’s say that H1 comes first in the race, then H2, then H3, then H4 and then H5 and similarly happens for the next races).
      Race 2: H6, H7, H8, H9, H10
      Race 3: H11, H12, H13, H14, H15
      Race 4: H16, H17, H18, H19, H20
      Race 5: H21, H22, H23, H24, H25

      For winners of all the above races arrange a new race.
      Race 6: H1, H6, H11, H16, H21
      So, now H1, H6, H11 are top 3 winners of Race 6. Then the horses H16 to H25 will not be there in top 3 fastest horses. After Race 6, we can conclude that H1 is the fastest horse among all. Now, we are interested in finding the second and third fastest horse.

      (i) For the second and third positions H2 and H3 could have the possibility but not H4 and H5.
      (ii) As H6 comes second in the race, so we can be sure that only H7 could have possibility of third fastest horse.
      (iii) As H11 comes third in the race, so we can be sure that H12 to H15 cannot acquire top 3 positions.
      Race 7: H2, H3, H6, H7, H11
      From Race 7, we will get second and third fastest horses.
      So, minimum number of races required to find 3 fastest horses is 7.
    3.   8
    4.   9
