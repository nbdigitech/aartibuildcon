  5. The sum of the third and the ninth term of an AP is 10. Find a possible sum of the first 11 terms of this AP?( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   44
    2.   50
    3.   66
    4.   55
      Explanation:
      Let, First term = a
      Common difference = d
      ∴ Third term = a +2d
      Ninth term = a + 8d
      Sum of the third and the ninth term = 10
      (a + 2d) + (a + 8d) = 10
      2a + 10d = 10
      Sum of n terms in an A.P=n/2 [2a+(n-1)d]
      ∴Sum of first 11 terms =11/2 [2a+(11-1)d]
      =11/2 [2a+10d]
      =11/2 (10)=55
