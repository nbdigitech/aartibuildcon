  11. How can we avoid cascading rollbacks?
    I.Using strict two phase locking protocol
    II.Using rigorous two phase protocol
    III.Using basic two phase locking protocol( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   I only
    2.   I and II only
      Explanation:
      We can avoid cascading rollbacks in strict schedules. In these schedules, transactions can neither read nor write an item X until the last transaction that wrote X has committed (or aborted). We know that strict 2PL and rigorous 2PL ensures strict schedules. Since, in strict 2PL, the transaction does not release any of its exclusive (write) locks until after it commits or aborts, and in rigorous 2PL, the transaction does not release any of its exclusive (write) locks or share (read) locks until after it commits or aborts.
    3.   II and III only
