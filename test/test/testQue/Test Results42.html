    3.   Mason : Stone

  10. When two dice are thrown simultaneously, what is the probability that the sum of two numbers that turn up is less than or equal to 10?( Marks: 0.00 )
    1.   7/12
    2.   5/6
    3.   1/12
    4.   11/12
      Explanation:
      Find the probability of sum being greater than 10.
      Combinations of two dice’s whose sum is 12 is (6,6).
      Combinations whose sum is 11 is (5,6) , (6,5).
      If you throw two dice’s then there are 36 occurrences. Out of 36 occurrences, 3 occurrences satisfy the condition.
      Therefore, probability whose sum of two numbers greater than or equal to 10
      =3/36 =1/12
      Hence probability that sum of two numbers is less or equal to 10 = 1-(1/12)= 11/12
